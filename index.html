Who I am

I am a professional design driven individual passionate about creating beautiful UI and Graphic designs and websites.

I have been in the graphic design business for over 15 years with experiece in Adobe photoshop, Corel draw, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premire Pro, Final Cut Pro, HTML, Javascript, jQuery, Android Studio, GitHub, Adobe XD, Cinema 4D, and Realflow.

Define

Define your project. Whether professional or personal your project should contain definitive details that outline exactly what, and how your project flows.

Design

The design process can sometimes be tedious, but every detail and attention to detail counts. Your audience must understand and comprehend the message you are protraying in your designs. My process is detailed oriented with emphasis on font type, creation of images, proper grammar, and an overall visual experience both you and your audience can enjoy.

Build

My build process is simply. My customers provide me with full details of the project. The projects origin, demographic, color scheme, font selection, page layout, custom graphics for me to create or provided graphic you the customer submit.

Launch

Before you launch, you are given two complimentary revisions. Ever detail, paragraph, color choice, font, column, art, images, etc. is checked and varified by the customer. Full tested on all media and across all platforms needed by the customer before your product ships.
